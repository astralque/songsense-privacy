SongSense Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Overview

SongSense is a music recognition and lyrics translation app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Audio Data: When you use the song recognition feature, we temporarily record a short audio sample (approximately 5 seconds) to identify the song. This audio is sent to our recognition service and is not stored permanently.

Device ID: We generate a random device identifier to save your listening history locally. This ID is not linked to your personal identity.

Usage Data: We store your song recognition history to provide you with quick access to previously identified songs and cached translations.

Third-Party Services

SongSense uses the following third-party services:

  • Shazam API - for music recognition
  • LRCLIB - for lyrics retrieval
  • DeepL - for lyrics translation
  • Supabase - for data storage

Data Storage

Your listening history and cached translations are stored securely in our database. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@songsense.app

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
